## Summary

This paper studies how scalable oversight performance changes as models, tasks, or oversight resources scale. It is part of DeepMind's broader effort to understand whether methods such as debate, verifier-based supervision, or related oversight schemes can remain informative as systems become more capable.

## Key points

- Puts empirical structure around the question of whether oversight can keep up with capability growth.
- Directly relates to debate-, prover-verifier-, and judge-based supervision strategies.
- Connects abstract alignment theory to measurable trends in model and evaluator performance.
