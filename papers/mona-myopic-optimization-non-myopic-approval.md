## Summary

MONA proposes a training approach that combines myopic optimization with a non-myopic approval signal in order to reduce multi-step reward hacking. The paper is part of DeepMind's broader effort to mitigate alignment failures that emerge over longer action horizons.

## Key points

- Targets long-horizon reward-hacking behavior rather than only single-step errors.
- Uses approval-style feedback to shape behavior more robustly across multi-step tasks.
- Provides a concrete training proposal within DeepMind's reward-hacking mitigation agenda.
